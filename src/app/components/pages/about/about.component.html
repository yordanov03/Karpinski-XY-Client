About me

Where I come from

I was born in Radom, Poland, and grew up in Tarnobrzeg. After earning my degree from Wroclaw University of Economics in 2011, I continued to expand my education and artistic horizons. In 2019, I graduated from the Eugeniusz Geppert Academy of Fine Arts and Design in Wroclaw, where I studied painting under the tutelage of Prof. Piotr Błażejewski and Dr. Anna Kołodziejczyk.

My artistic work spans various mediums—from drawing and painting to scetches and architecturial design. In parallel to my life in art, I have built a career in finance, where I currently work as a financial controller for a multinational company.

The process

Allow me to walk you through my painting process

The creation of a painting is a journey of transformation and expression, where an initial spark of inspiration evolves through various stages into a captivating piece of art. Each step, from the selection of colors to the meticulous layering, plays a vital role in bringing the artist's vision to life, crafting a visual narrative that speaks both to the creator and the audience.

Inspiration

The journey begins with a spark of inspiration, where ideas and emotions blend to form the concept of the artwork

Color Selection

This stage involves carefully choosing a palette, where each color is selected for its ability to convey the intended mood and atmosphere.

Shape Outlining

Here, the initial outlines and shapes are drawn, laying the foundation for the painting's structure and composition.

Layers

This crucial phase involves applying layers of paint, each adding depth, texture, and richness to the composition, enhancing the visual impact of the artwork.

Finishing touches

In the final stage, detailed elements and refinements are added, bringing the painting to life with its full depth and vibrancy.
