I see the world differently

I embrace a perspective that reveals the hidden wonders in the familiar, transforming the everyday into a canvas of vibrant possibilities. My artistry is a journey through a kaleidoscope of realities. I capture the essence of the unseen, weaving the subtle threads of the ordinary into a tapestry of unexpected shapes and colors.

About Me

A financial expert by day and artist by night

I was born in Radom, Poland, and grew up in Tarnobrzeg. After earning my degree from Wroclaw University of Economics in 2011, I continued to expand my education and artistic horizons. In 2019, I graduated from the Eugeniusz Geppert Academy of Fine Arts and Design in Wroclaw, where I studied painting under the tutelage of Prof. Piotr Błażejewski and Dr. Anna Kołodziejczyk.

My artistic work spans various mediums—from drawing and painting to scetches and architecturial design. In parallel to my life in art, I have built a career in finance, where I currently work as a financial controller for a multinational company.
